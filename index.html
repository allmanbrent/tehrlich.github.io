Tyler Ehrlich

I am a recent graduate of Cornell University interested in the intersection of music and technology. I studied conducting with Cynthia Johnston Turner; we're now working to incorporate Google Glass effectively into rehearsal and performance contexts. Last semester, I directed the pit orchestras for Sweeney Todd and Company. Finally, I TAed for two courses, Conducting and Introductory Design and Programming for the Web.

When not making music and programming, I enjoy photography, sailing, skiing, and scuba diving.
