Tyler Ehrlich

I'm a senior at Cornell University interested in the intersection of music and technology. I study conducting with Cynthia Johnston Turner; we're working to incorporate Google Glass into rehearsals and performances. This semester, I'm directing the pit orchestras for both Sweeney Todd and Company. Finally, I TA for two courses, Conducting and Introductory Design and Programming for the Web.

In my free time, I enjoy photography, sailing, and scuba diving.
